Bayerische Motoren Werke AG or BMW

a German automobile and aircraft engine concern and the eponymous brand of premium and luxury cars and motorcycles. The concern is the parent company (holding) of the BMW Group association, which includes the following separate productions: BMW motorcycles, cars of the BMW, Rolls-Royce, MINI brands, subsidiary productions of BMW M, BMW i. In the Forbes Global 2000 list of the world's largest public companies for 2022, the BMW Group took 64th place, and in the Fortune Global 500 list - 59th place.
BMW was founded by Carl Friedrich Rapp in October 1916, initially as an aircraft engine manufacturer, Bayerische Flugzeug-Werke. The district of Munich, Milbertshofen, was chosen as the place of business because it was home to the Flugmaschinenfabrik of Gustav Otto, a German aircraft manufacturer.

BMW EFFICIENT DYNAMICS.

Less fuel consumption. More driving pleasure. Clear driving pleasure thanks to maximum performance and minimum fuel consumption. BMW EfficientDynamics is the name of BMW's strategy to minimize fuel consumption and CO2 emissions, while at the same time increasing dynamics and driving pleasure. Thanks to intelligent energy management and lightweight construction, as well as aerodynamics and engine efficiency, sustainable mobility is already a standard feature in every BMW today. Further innovations include new forms of drive system, such as the innovative BMW eDrive technology.
